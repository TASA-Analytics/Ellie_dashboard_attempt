About This Dataset

This tool provides supply-chain emissions intensity factors across Scope 1, 2, and 3 categories for multiple countries and industry sectors. Factors are expressed in kilograms of CO₂ equivalent per US dollar of economic output (kgCO₂e/USD).

Sectors are classified using both NAICS (North American Industry Classification System) and ISIC Rev. 4 (International Standard Industrial Classification) codes to ensure international comparability.

Data is updated annually and reflects the most recent available input-output modelling results. Factors support spend-based emissions accounting in line with the GHG Protocol Corporate Value Chain Standard.

1
Scope 1 — Direct Emissions

Emissions from sources owned or controlled by the organisation — combustion, process emissions, fugitive releases.

2
Scope 2 — Purchased Energy

Indirect emissions from the generation of purchased electricity, steam, heat or cooling consumed by the organisation.

3
Scope 3 — Value Chain

All other indirect emissions in the value chain — upstream supply chain, downstream use, and end-of-life treatment.
